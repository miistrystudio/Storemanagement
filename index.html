Welcome to MistryStudio!

This app helps you manage your stationery shop. Here is what each section does:

📊 Dashboard

See a quick summary of your shop — total products, stock value, today's sales, and profit.

📦 Products

Add all your shop items here. Enter name, category, cost price, selling price, and stock.

🏪 Inventory

Check which items are running low. Use + and − buttons to update stock quickly.

📅 Daily Tracker

Record every sale and expense for each day. The app calculates profit automatically.

🏷️ Categories

Go to Products → click "Categories" to add or remove categories.

💾 Export / Import

Download a backup or restore from one. Always keep a backup!

💡 Tip: Start by adding your categories, then add all products, and then record daily sales!
